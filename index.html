South Carolina

South Carolina is a state located on the eastern coast of the United States of America. It is bordered by Georgia and North Carolina from the north and south, and the Atlantic Ocean to the east. It's population is 5.1 million people, with the populus being spread evenly across the state. There are multiple major cities, including; Greenville, Spartanburg, Columbia, Charleston, and Myrtle Beach.

South Carolina is unequivically one of the most beautiful states in the United States! It has beaches, mountains, and so many historical sites that date back hundreds of years! Check out more about all there is to do on the individual city pages!
